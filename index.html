Welcome to ClickMaster

Challenge yourself and test your clicking speed with ClickMaster! Can you click as fast as possible in just 10 seconds? Find out now!

How it works:

  1. Click the "INCREMENT" button to begin the 10-second countdown.
  2. Click as many times as you can within the time limit.
  3. When the timer reaches zero, your clicking speed will be displayed.

Compete with friends, track your progress, and see who can achieve the highest clicks! ClickMaster is a fun and interactive way to measure your clicking skills.
